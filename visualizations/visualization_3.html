Cloudiness


This graph shows the distribution of cloudiness percentage at different latitudes. It is clear that the most common reading is 0%, at all latitudes, with the next most at about 75% and then 95%. It is also apparent that the higher concentrations of cloudiness are at the 0 - 70 deg. latitudes. This is most like due to the higher temperatures around the equator and tropics above the equator.
